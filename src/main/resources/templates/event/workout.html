2023 Hero Workout

April 8, 2023

10:00 AM-12noon

Bare Hands Crossfit

618 N Main Street, Columbia, IL

The Maupin HERO Workout (WOD) is in remembrance of SSG Matt Maupin of Batavia, OH. Matt was taken POW during the Iraq War in 2004. His remains were unfortunately uncovered in 2008. The founder of Songs4Soldiers served with Matt and survived the attack. Per Matt’s mom’s request, 100% of the proceeds of this event will go to Songs4Soldiers aiding them in their mission of helping fellow combat veterans!

In-person ticket sales and registration begins at 9 am. Please come early to stretch. The workout begins promptly at 10 am!

Open to the public and ALL fitness levels. Scaled versions of the workout available. There will be plenty of outdoor and indoor space available so bring your friends! Post workout beer!
